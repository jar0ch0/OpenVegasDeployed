WHY NOW

The agent era needs a financial layer.

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang recently described a future where 75,000 humans could work alongside 7.5 million AI agents, a 100:1 ratio. If that world arrives, agents will need more than models. They will need balances, budget controls, replenishment logic, and continuity infrastructure. OpenVegas is building toward that layer.
