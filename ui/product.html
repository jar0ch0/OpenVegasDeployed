PRODUCT OVERVIEW

A wallet layer for AI usage.

OpenVegas gives users a simple way to hold value, top up balances, and keep AI workflows running. What starts as a better credit experience becomes a broader control layer for usage, thresholds, and replenishment.

WHAT THE PRODUCT DOES

OpenVegas combines four primitives into one surface:

  • Wallet-backed balances
  • Clean top-up and payment flows
  • Usage continuity when budgets get tight
  • A future path to agent-native budget controls
WHY USERS CARE

Less friction. More runtime.

The problem is not just paying for AI. The problem is what happens when funding breaks the flow. OpenVegas is designed to reduce those interruptions and make AI usage feel continuous.

HUMAN FIRST, AGENT NEXT

Built for people now. Built for agents next.

Today, OpenVegas helps human users manage AI credit balances. Over time, the same system can support autonomous agents that need operating budgets, refill thresholds, and policy-aware limits.
