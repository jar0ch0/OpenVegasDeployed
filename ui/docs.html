ABOUT

OpenVegas is a wallet-backed platform for funding, managing, and extending AI usage. It starts by making balances, top-ups, and billing continuity better for people. Over time, it expands into the control layer autonomous agents will need to operate with real budgets, limits, and replenishment logic.

YC STYLE

OpenVegas is building the wallet and replenishment layer for AI usage. Today, users hit friction from fragmented top-ups, scattered balances, and hard budget stops. We turn that into a cleaner balance-driven system with stronger retention. Over time, the same infrastructure becomes a budget and treasury layer for autonomous agents.
